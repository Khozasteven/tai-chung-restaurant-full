About Tai Chung Restaurant

1997

Tai Chung: Your Halal Gateway to Authentic Chinese Flavors in Cape Town

Since 1997, Tai Chung Restaurant has been a culinary landmark in Athlone, Cape Town. Founded by Natasha Chung, a visionary with a passion for authentic Chinese cuisine and a deep respect for Halal traditions, Tai Chung has become synonymous with takeaway excellence.

Natasha's unwavering commitment to quality and tradition is evident in every dish. Using only the finest Halal ingredients and time-honored recipes, Tai Chung delivers an unforgettable culinary experience, right to your doorstep.

Tai Chung's Takeaway Trinity: Chow Mein, Spring Rolls, and Chop Suey

While Tai Chung's menu is a treasure trove of Chinese delicacies, three dishes have consistently topped the takeaway charts:

Chow Mein: A takeaway classic, Tai Chung's Chow Mein is a symphony of flavors and textures. Whether you prefer the savory delight of chicken, the tender succulence of beef, or the exquisite taste of prawns, each bite is a journey to the heart of China.

Spring Rolls: These golden parcels of goodness are a takeaway must-have. The vegetarian Spring Rolls offer a delightful crunch filled with fresh vegetables, while the chicken Spring Rolls tantalize with a hint of spice. Perfect for sharing or savoring solo, they are a testament to Tai Chung's dedication to crafting unique and unforgettable flavors.

Chop Suey: Experience the essence of Chinese culinary tradition with Tai Chung's Chop Suey. With a choice of tender beef, succulent calamari, tender chicken, or plump prawns, this dish is a takeaway adventure. The rich aroma and authentic taste will transport you to the bustling streets of China, all from the comfort of your home.

The Tai Chung Takeaway Promise: Quality You Can Trust, Flavor You'll Crave

At Tai Chung, takeaway is not just about convenience; it's about bringing the restaurant experience to you. It's about enjoying authentic Chinese Halal food, made with passion and precision, in the comfort of your own space.

Ready to tantalize your taste buds? Order your Tai Chung takeaway today and discover a world of flavor, delivered right to your door.
